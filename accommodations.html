--- layout: page title: Accommodations ---

We have blocked off rooms in a number of hotels near the wedding venue. We chose these hotels because they cover a range of room rates while still being close to the wedding venue. It should be noted that there are many, many other hotels in the city to choose from if one wants to trade distance for cost.

As an alternative to staying in a Manhattan hotel, one could book a cheaper hotel in Jersey City and take the PATH train accross the river to the Christopher St. PATH station which is within walking distance to the wedding venue.
