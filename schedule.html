Getting to Houston Hall

Houston Hall is a beergarden located in Manhattan's West Village neighborhood. We do not suggest driving your own car as there is no guaranteed parking. Thankfully, NYC has excellent public transportation and cabs!

Address: 222 W Houston St New York, NY 10014

By Subway

Houston Hall is right next to the Houston St. 1 train stop. Upon leaving the station, walk east down Houston St, and Houston Hall is midway down the block on the left. Alternatively, the West 4 St. Station is a 6 minute walk away and serves the A, B, C, D, E, F, and M lines. Upon leaving the West 4 St. Station, walk south down 6th Avenue, and then hang a right on Houston St. Houston Hall will be midway down the block on the right.

By Taxi

Just tell the cab driver that you're going to Houston and Varick Street, and you should be good to go!
